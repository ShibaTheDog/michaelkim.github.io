Here is visualizations made for Assignment 3!

Visualization 1: Global sales by Genre and Platform

This data visualization shows the sales of game titles by genre and platform through a heatmap, with tan-ish colors representing a low sale and red colors representing a high sales value.

Visualization 2: Sales Over Time by Platform and Genre

This data visualization shows the sales of titles in terms of platforms and genres from 1980-2020. Platforms are represented through colors on the bar chart while genres are represented through the stacked bar charts.

Visualization 3: Regional Sales vs. Platform

This data visualization shows a stacked bar chart that compares the different regional sales across each genre. Bars are stacked ontop of each other to help give quick and fast comparisons.

Visualization 4: How well did the top 3 publishers do against Nintendo in terms of global sales?

This data visualization compares the top 3 publishers (which are Activision, Namco Bandai Games, and Electronic arts) against Nintendo, one of the bestselling game title creators. Using the nature of quick comparisons of a stacked bar chart, users can quickly make comparisons about which companies performed the best for each year and if Nintendo had more sales compared to the top 3 publishers.
